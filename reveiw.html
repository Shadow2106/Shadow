Romeo and Juliet

Author: William Shakespeare

Genre: Romance

Synopsis: In Verona, Italy, Romeo Montague and Juliet Capulet, young lovers from feuding families, fall deeply in love. Their secret romance leads to tragic events, culminating in their untimely deaths, which finally reconcile their warring families.

About the Author

William Shakespeare (1564–1616) was an English playwright and poet, known as the "Bard of Avon." He wrote 39 plays, 154 sonnets, and numerous poems, exploring timeless themes of love, power, and betrayal. His works remain influential in literature and theater worldwide.
